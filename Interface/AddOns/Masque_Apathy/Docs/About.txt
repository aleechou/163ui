Apathy is a skin for Masque.

Resources:

  Project Page: http://www.wowace.com/projects/masque_apathy
  Masque: http://www.wowace.com/projects/masque

Installation:

  1. Download the package.
  2. Extract the package to your "World of Warcraft\Interface\AddOns" directory.

  Note: You must have Masque installed to use this add-on.
